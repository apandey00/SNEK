Adoption Page

Here you can find all the snakes and animals that are up for adoption right now! These animals are desperate for a home and you could be the lucky one to take them home. We have just taken in some new snakes from the Galapagos islands so be sure to check them out before they're gone!

  • Name: Jimmy
  • Age: 25
  • Species: Boa Constrictor
  • Favourite Activity: Sunbathing
  • Jimmy is a special snake. He is one of our newest members from South America and is sure to go quickly so snap him up if he is what you look for in a scaly friend! Jimmy loves to lie in the sun and suffocate small mammals for food, but he also has a playful side and loves to snuggle up to any future owners.
